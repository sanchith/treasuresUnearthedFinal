TREASURES

Since the beginning of time, minerals have been around the earth in many forms. Nowadays the use of minerals is broadly ranged, different industries like medicine, chemistry, jewelry, and even home accessories are using minerals more and more. Quartz and geodes are very popular for many of these applications.

Jewelry

Minerals create beautiful stones that can be used to make a wide array of jewelry like necklaces, rings and bracelets.

Decor

The geodes and stones are also used to make beautiful pieces of art, and home accessories.

Commercial

Minerals are not just a pretty face! They have been used for many years in different applications like paint, batteries, and even cellphones!.
